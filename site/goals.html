Our Goals

 

Short Term Goals

 

1. Supporting Pastoral care

2. Providing nutritious food for children and elderly

3. Building elderly homes

4. Providing drinking water through tankers

5. Learning Centers for High School Students

6. Evening schools for Women

 

Long Term Goals

 

1. Expand educational learning centers

2. Providing clean drinking water facilities

3. Creating alternative employment sources

4. Collaborate with other likeminded service organizations

5. Provide agricultural infrastructure support

6. Provide higher educational opportunities

7. Strive to build model villages

8. Construct Medical clinics and Mobile clinics

9. Providing clean sanitation facilities

10. Eradication of Malaria and HIV/AIDS

11. Alternate energy sources – Solar, Gas and Biogas etc

 


A word from Founder

I can’t think of many people more helpless than school children from vulnerable populations in rural India. We can’t help every child in India, but we can reach a few.  These children have the capacities to be like you and me, but they do not have the opportunities to excel, unlike the affluent next door in the same village.  Some one gave a push to my great grandfather in the 19th century -- an illiterate person with loin cloth, who could become a teacher in the same village, and he could be able to send his son to Madras Christian College (an elite institution in India) and who then became the first Indian Principal of a high school in Rajahmundry.  His great grandchild is now the associate provost at a high research state university, and his children are doing very well in the United States.  This was all possible because of Him through some one lifting their little finger toward us.  Please keep praying for these initiatives, and also spread the word so that we have more partners to work in His vineyard

--Dr James Samuel Maddirala
