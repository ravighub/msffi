For verily I say unto you, If ye have faith as a grain of mustard seed, ye shall say unto this mountain, Remove hence to yonder place; and it shall remove; and nothing shall be impossible unto you...Matthew 17:20

Mustard Seed Faith Foundation's purpose is to strive to change the lives of rural communities one at a time. MSFF strives to "raise leaders, who raise families, who raise the His kingdom here on earth," and empower them to become self sustaining.

More..

OUR SHORT TERM GOALS:

1. Supporting Pastoral care

2. Providing nutritious food for children and elderly

3. Building elderly homes

4. Providing drinking water through tankers

5. Learning Centers for High School Students

6. Evening schools for Women

OUR LONG TERM GOALS:

1. Expand educational learning centers

2. Providing clean drinking water facilities

3. Creating alternative employment sources

4. Collaborate with other likeminded service organizations

5. Provide agricultural infrastructure support

6. Provide higher educational opportunities

7. Strive to build model villages

8. Construct Medical clinics and Mobile clinics

9. Providing clean sanitation facilities

10. Eradication of Malaria and HIV/AIDS

11. Alternate energy sources – Solar, Gas and Biogas etc

MSFF programs and initiatives belong to the communities so that they can derive a sense of pride and satisfaction in knowing that they can care for their own people. MSFF is dedicated to the improvement of these communities in providing opportunities for economic viability to people who lack opportunity to break out of the cycle of poverty which grips their lives.

MSFF began in March 2011 in Madison, Mississippi, USA as a faith-based community of families and friends committed to actively caring for, nurturing and uplifting of the vulnerable and marginalized populations, forgotten by society through offering creative outreach programs in the rural areas.

Inspired by the serving and caring ministry of Jesus, we aim to uplift the most vulnerable populations, especially rural poor and marginalized communities through caring, sharing and training. We are committed to fostering these communities, with loving and mutual respect, with the intention of strengthening their faith, joy, hope, and dignity.
