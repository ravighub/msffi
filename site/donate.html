Our Services

There are teeming million vulnerable populations in India, and God compels us to show our mustard seed faith in shedding light in their lives. In connection to this thought, what can we do? How can we show mustard seed faith? How can we enable ourselves to keep lives on the lamp stand, not under the bed? There are opportunities to serve that we identified. Please consider prayerfully in order to make difference in the lives of few people through our committed services

Get Involved

All indigenous ministries and the missionaries that work with them are in need of your prayers. They fight many spiritual battles, and the prayers of God’s people on their behalf are of immeasurable value. We believe that God has brought each and every one of us into community with one another and has equipped us for a specific function within that community. It is our desire to see everyone discover where they fit and enable them to live it out!
