Core Values

 

People - Any service organization, is nothing more or less than the people who make it up.

Service - This is about effort and service. We all have a task to do, and we are here to provide value to our communities where we render our service.

Together – We will pull together, and share knowledge, to become stronger.

One - We all have a shared destiny. We will strive to become “One” by looking for common solutions to problems, and sharing facilities and resources.

Global-World – We will reflect global-world backgrounds and global-world experiences.

Leadership - we are here to lead, be the best and nothing less.

 

Dr. James Samuel Maddirala is the Founder of MSFF. He is described as a person who is deeply spiritual and caring, with boundless energy and charisma. No obstacle is too great for him when it comes to help.

 

Because of Him through you

 

There are teeming million vulnerable populations in India, and God compels us to show our mustard seed faith in shedding light in their lives. In connection to this thought, what can we do? How can we show mustard seed faith? How can we enable ourselves to keep lives on the lamp stand, not under the bed? There are opportunities to serve that we identified. Please consider prayerfully in order to make difference in the lives of few people through our committed services.

 


A word from Founder

I can’t think of many people more helpless than school children from vulnerable populations in rural India. We can’t help every child in India, but we can reach a few.  These children have the capacities to be like you and me, but they do not have the opportunities to excel, unlike the affluent next door in the same village.  Some one gave a push to my great grandfather in the 19th century -- an illiterate person with loin cloth, who could become a teacher in the same village, and he could be able to send his son to Madras Christian College (an elite institution in India) and who then became the first Indian Principal of a high school in Rajahmundry.  His great grandchild is now the associate provost at a high research state university, and his children are doing very well in the United States.  This was all possible because of Him through some one lifting their little finger toward us.  Please keep praying for these initiatives, and also spread the word so that we have more partners to work in His vineyard

--Dr James Samuel Maddirala
